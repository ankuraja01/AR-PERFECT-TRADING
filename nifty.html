About NIFTY50

NIFTY50 is India's leading stock market index representing the top 50 companies listed on the NSE (National Stock Exchange). It covers major sectors like banking, IT, pharma, FMCG, and more.

Traders use NIFTY as a benchmark to track market performance and analyze trends for short- and long-term investing.
